About Us

(Shree Ganesh Bakery Nandani Pvt. Ltd.)

Chakote Group of Industries is a 32-years experienced group and one of the leading manufacturers of specialised Indian Bakery and Indian ethnic & Shahi Kolhapuri namkeen products in India. Operating from our head office in Nandani, Kolhapur and state-of-the-art production facilities in Kolhapur and Latur.

Chakote has created and developed high-quality Indian Bakery and Indian ethnic namkeen products that are economical and affordable for everyone. We are committed to providing our customers and consumers with consistently good quality products that conform to Food Safety Standards (FSSAI), ISO 22000, GMP, and other certifications.

Chakote has a highly experienced and qualified research and development team that is always involved in new product development and keeps an eye on current industry trends.

We have a very strong brand acceptance in the states of Maharashtra, Karnataka, Goa, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, and Gujarat through our more than 1200 business partners, distributors, and stockists. Our products are available at millions of retail outlets in India.
